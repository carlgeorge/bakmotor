    Driver Profile

    Jay Callaway

    Pro Ultr4 driver Jay Callaway is a Dirt Riot Champion, Ultra4 Podium Winner and King of the Hammers EMC top 15 finisher. As a racer on a National stage, Ja has developed a social media footprint that has gained over 1,500,000 views.  Jay works relentlessly both behind the wheel and behind the scenes in his driving and marketing efferts to stand out, bringing an enormous amount of viewership towards his marketing partners.

    The BAK Motorsports Racing team sets itself apart by being centered on "Handsome Jay!" A driver who is more focused on his hair care than shock tuning.  A driver in the endless search for a custom leather race suit.  Our fans gravitate towards BAK Motorsports because we have found a way to be different - not just another guy in a helmet.  With 6 seasons of racing under the hood, Jay continues to push the envelope in the hardest racing series on the planet.
