    BAK Motorsports

    Bradley Keeter

    BAK Motorsports is a proud sponsor of the #4410 off-road race car competing in the Ultra4 and Best in the Desert racing series. Owned and operated by Bradley Keeter, BAK Motorsports supports athletes in off-road racing, golf and bass fishing. In 2015, BAK partnered with driver Jay Callaway to bring an East Coast force to the off-road racing world.

    Driver Profile

    Jay Callaway

    As an off-road racer on a international stage, Jay Callaway is the driver of the #4410 race car. He competes in the toughest off-road races on the planet for the Ultra4 and Best in the Desert race series, including King of the Hammers and the legendary Mint400. With 15 years of road racing under his belt, Callaway began off-road racing in 2011. He is a Dirt Riot Southeast Champion, Ultra4 Racing podium winner and King of the Hammers Every Man Challenge Top 15 finisher. In addition to BAK Motorsports, Callaway is proudly sponsored by Rugged Radios, RuffStuff Specialties, Lucky Pawn GA, Spidertrax Off-road, Carolina Auto Concepts, Factor55 and BF Goodrich Tires.

    Follow #HandsomeJay on Instagram, Facebook and Twitter.
